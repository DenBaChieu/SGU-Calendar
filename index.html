SGU Calendar

Automatically converts your SGU timetable into events in Google Calendar

How to use:

  1. Go to SGU website
  2. Login using your student account
  3. Click on "Thời khóa biểu dạng học kì"
  4. Press Ctrl + A then Ctrl + C
  5. Paste the copied content into the input box below
